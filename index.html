A surgeon watched her patients struggle with the simplest daily routines — brushing their teeth, holding a spoon, gripping a pen. Not because they lacked the will, but because nothing was designed for their hands.

So she built something that was.

Umpra is a wellness product designed to give people with limited grip strength the ability to take care of themselves — independently, comfortably, and with dignity. Clinically tested with 40 patients. No bulky equipment. No complicated setups. Just a quiet, thoughtful solution for the moments that matter most.

Who It's For

Umpra is for anyone who wants to hold onto their daily routine — stroke survivors regaining movement, grandparents maintaining their independence, children building motor skills, and anyone living with arthritis or limited hand mobility.

It's also for the people who care for them.
